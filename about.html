About Us

Origin Story

Mr. Bho moved to Las Vegas from Thailand 40 years and brought his families delicious recipes with him. He opened his first location on E. Centennial in 1988 and has since been able to expand to many locations, all of which are loved by Las Vegas locals and tourists alike. We have four locations conveniently located all around the Las Vegas valley.
